Review Analyzer: Unveiling Sentiments and Topics

Details For Use:

The application analyzes the sentiment of each review, providing insights into its positive or negative nature. Users can gain valuable insights about the reviewed media product by understanding the overall sentiment associated with the reviews. Additionally, the application incorporates topic modeling, enabling users to explore two distinct topics within the reviews and delve deeper into the themes and discussions. It is important to ensure that there are at least 10 reviews for sufficient topic analysis.
